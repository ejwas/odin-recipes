Pierogi

Back to Home

Description

A traditional Polish dish consisting of dumplings filled with various ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup cheese (e.g., cottage cheese, cheddar)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • Butter, for frying

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, water, and salt to form a dough.
  2. Roll out the dough and cut into circles.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine mashed potatoes, cheese, and onion.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling on each dough circle and fold in half.
  5. Boil pierogi in salted water until they float.
  6. In a skillet, melt butter and fry the boiled pierogi until golden.
