Spaghetti

Back to Home

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish with a rich tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1/2 lb ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Pour in the marinara sauce and simmer for 10-15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and combine it with the sauce in the skillet.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.
