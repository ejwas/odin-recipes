Sushi

Description

A delicious sushi roll with fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sushi rice
  • 1/4 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 lb sushi-grade fish (e.g., tuna, salmon)
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, julienned
  • Nori (seaweed) sheets
  • Soy sauce, for serving

Steps

  1. Rinse the sushi rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  3. Once cooked, transfer the rice to a bowl and mix in the rice vinegar.
  4. Lay a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat.
  5. Spread a thin layer of sushi rice over the nori, leaving a small border at the top.
  6. Arrange the fish, avocado, and cucumber on top of the rice.
  7. Roll the sushi tightly using the mat, sealing the edge with a little water.
  8. Slice the roll into bite-sized pieces and serve with soy sauce.
