Lasagna

Back to Home

Description

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Fresh basil, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the beef and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the mozzarella cheese.
  6. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, and meat sauce, repeating until all ingredients are used.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake for 30-40 minutes, until bubbly and golden.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil before serving.
