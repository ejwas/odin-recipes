Description

A delicious burrito filled with beans, rice, and fresh vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 large flour tortilla
  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 avocado, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish

Steps

  1. Warm the tortilla in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Layer the rice, beans, salsa, cheese, and avocado on the tortilla.
  3. Roll the tortilla tightly, folding in the sides as you go.
  4. Slice the burrito in half and serve with cilantro on top.
